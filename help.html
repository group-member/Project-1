Feedback and Rating

We value your feedback! Click the Feedback button to share your thoughts on our website and rate your experience. Your input helps us improve the system and tailor it to better meet your needs. Please be honest and detailed, and feel free to suggest any improvements.

Live Stream Monitoring

Our Live Stream feature allows you to remotely view how robotic systems, which are not part of our monitoring network, are handling operations. This feature is crucial in detecting real-time garbage sorting accuracy. It helps in identifying whether sorting is done correctly or if errors have occurred. You can observe the live footage and gain insights into the operational process.

Upload Image for Detection

Similar to live stream monitoring, the Upload Image feature enables you to upload images for real-time garbage detection. This tool will analyze whether the sorting process was done correctly or not based on the provided image. This helps us continuously improve the system by detecting sorting issues that may otherwise go unnoticed.

Data Log and Report Generation

The Data Log page allows you to view and generate reports from the data detected previously. This feature provides a comprehensive overview of all monitored data. You can download the reports in either PDF or CSV format for further analysis and record-keeping. This feature is useful for generating historical reports on system performance or reviewing specific periods of operation.
